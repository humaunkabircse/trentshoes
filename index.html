About Us

Located just an hour away from the Dhaka International Airport, our factory stands erect in Savar, Ashulia, with sufficient space to house our valuable workers and machines. All financial and management decisions are administered by highly qualified expatriate technicians. Each and every one of our industrial systems are maintained at world class standards. Additionally, we have set up heavy duty backup generators to ensure punctuality. Our production processes are in compliance with all current international specifications. We also have in-house arrangements to accommodate our clients along with travelling options in case they wish to check and make sure everything meets their standards.
